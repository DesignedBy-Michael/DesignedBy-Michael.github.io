How to make Honey Muffins
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking soda in a small bowl
  2. Mix eggs, honey, oat milk and vanilla in a seperate larger bowl
  3. Add the dry mixture to the liquid one in two batches, mixing throughly
  4. Distrubute mixture into greased muffin tin
  5. Bake for 16 mins, or until lightly browned
  6. Enjoy.
